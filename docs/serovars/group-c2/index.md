# Group C2-C3 (O:8)

Serovars in this group share the O:8 somatic antigen.

| Serovar | Antigenic Formula | Primary Animal Reservoir |
|---|---|---|
| [*S.* Albany](albany.md) | `8,20:z4,z24:-` | Serovar Albany was mainly found in chicken but was also present in turkey, ducks, pigs, cattle, and captive zoo wild animals. |
| [*S.* Altona](altona.md) | `8,20:r,[i]:z6` | Serovar Altona was mainly found inchickens but was also present in cattle, pigs, and horses |
| [*S.* Blockley](blockley.md) | `6,8:k:1,5` | Chickens are the main reservoir for serovar Blockley. |
| [*S.* Bovismorbificans](bovismorbificans.md) | `6,8,20:r,[i]:1,5` | Pigs tend to be the main animal reservoir for serovar Bovismorbificans. |
| [*S.* Corvallis](corvallis.md) | `8,20:z4,z23:[z6]` | Serovar Corvallis was found to be associated withpigs in South-East Asia. |
| [*S.* Goldcoast](goldcoast.md) | `6,8:r:l,w` | Serovar Goldcoast has been isolated from swine and cattle. |
| [*S.* Hadar](hadar.md) | `6,8:z10:e,n,x` | The main reservoirs of serovar Hadar are turkey flocks and turkey meat. |
| [*S.* Kentucky](kentucky.md) | `8,20:i:z6` | : Chicken is the main reservoir for serovar Kentucky. |
| [*S.* Kottbus](kottbus.md) | `6,8:e,h:1,5` | Poultry including chicken and turkey may be potential reservoirs for serovar Kottbus. |
| [*S.* Litchfield](litchfield.md) | `6,8:l,v:1,2` | Poultry and turtles may be potential hosts. |
| [*S.* Manhattan](manhattan.md) | `6,8:d:1,5` | Pigs and chickens are potential reservoirs for serovar Manhattan. |
| [*S.* Muenchen](muenchen.md) | `6,8:d:1,2` | Swine, cattle, and poultry are reservoirs for serovar Muenchen. |
| [*S.* Newport](newport.md) | `6,8,20:e,h:1,2` | Cattle is the main reservoir for serovar Newport. |
